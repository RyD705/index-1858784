    Covid-19 introductions

    • Beginning in December 2019 a strange virus was found in Wuhan China.
    • This virus knowns as Covid-19 started to spread all around the world quickly.
    • Positive cases started to rise quickly; people got hospitalized and even die.
    • Scientist started to research the virus; figure out to test the infection; how to treat the patient.
    • Recently Covid-19 vaccine successfully passed several clinical trials and released to the public.

    Covid-19 reserch timeline

    back
    • Research
      • Basic Research
      • Drug Discovery
      • Preclinical Research
      • Clinical Research
      • Approved Drug
    • Development
      • MPI and Cepi worked with governments to develop the vaccine.
      • Studies have shown that past vaccines started to fail. They hope that covid-19 vaccine a success
    • Clinical trials
      • Pre-clinical: vaccine tested on animals
      • Phase 1: vaccine tested on small group of people, usually between 10-50.
      • Phase 2 test: vaccine tested on hundreds of human volunteers to further check safety,side effects,immune response and dosage.
      • Phase 3 test: vaccine tested on thousands of people to determine whether its effective and to monitor safety.
    • CDC approval - CDC regulators review the result to decide whether to approve the vaccine for use; licensing and manufacturing.
    • Release to public
